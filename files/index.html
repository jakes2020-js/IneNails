01 — Philosophy

The art of doing less, beautifully.

At IneNails, we treat each set of nails as a small, slow ritual — never a transaction.

We trained in the Japanese tradition of ‘teinei’ — meticulous care — and apply it to every step. Tools are sterilized after every guest. Polish is sourced from the most respected ateliers in Tokyo and Seoul. We use only allergy-conscious, low-odor formulas, and we take the time to understand each client’s nail health before lifting a brush.

The result: nails that wear longer, look softer, and never come at the cost of your skin or your peace of mind.

04 — Hygiene & Safety

Cleanliness is the quietest luxury.

  • Autoclave-Sterilized Instruments

    Every metal tool is sterilized at 134°C between guests. We hold logs available on request.

07 — Studio Policies

Mutual respect, clearly written.

  • 01

    Late Arrivals

    A 10-minute grace period is built into every appointment. Beyond that, your service may be shortened so we can honor the next guest's time. Past 20 minutes, the booking is treated as a no-show.

  • 02

    No-Shows & Cancellations

    No-shows are non-refundable and must be rescheduled with a new deposit. Out of fairness to our team and other guests, this policy is firm.

  • 03

    Medical Emergencies

    The above does not apply to genuine medical emergencies. With written documentation (a doctor's note or hospital record), we waive any fees and reschedule with care.

  • 04

    Nail Health First

    If we detect infection, fungal involvement, or significant damage during inspection, we will not perform service. Your deposit converts to credit toward a future visit once your nails have healed.

  • 05

    Allergy Disclosure

    Please share known sensitivities at booking. We will run a free patch test 48 hours before your appointment if needed.

09 — Questions

Anything else?

What does the free nail inspection involve?

Before any service, we examine your natural nails for signs of infection, allergic reaction, fungal change, or damage. It takes about five minutes and is included with every appointment, no matter the service booked.

What products do you use?

Polish and gel are imported from established Japanese houses (such as Bio Sculpture Japan and Presto) and Korean ateliers (such as Gellipop and Tiny). All products are HEMA-free and lower-odor than typical Western brands.

What if I have a nail allergy?

Tell us at booking. We can run a free 48-hour patch test before your appointment with the exact products we plan to use, and we keep a hypoallergenic line for guests with confirmed sensitivities.

What happens if I miss my appointment?

No-shows are non-refundable and require a new deposit to rebook. The exception is documented medical emergencies — bring a doctor's note and we'll waive any fees and reschedule with care.

Do you work on infected or damaged nails?

No — and we say so kindly. Performing service over an active infection can worsen it and risk spreading it to other guests. We'll refer you to a healthcare provider, hold your deposit as credit, and welcome you back when your nails have healed.

Are your technicians certified?

Every IneNails artist holds JNA Level 2 or higher certification (or international equivalents) and completes continuing education each quarter. We're happy to share credentials on request.

How long does a typical appointment take?

Anywhere from 40 minutes for a removal to 120 minutes for sculpted extensions. We schedule generously so we never rush — but we keep tight time so we never run over into your day.
